Key Themes
AI has moved from discretionary experiment to core line item — budgets reflect strategic commitment, not pilot-stage exploration
IT spend is being both expanded and reallocated — traditional categories are funding the shift toward AI-first infrastructure
Incumbents face near-term budget pressure without demonstrating AI-driven ROI to justify renewal
Infrastructure and security benefit as enabling layers — cloud and security spend grow alongside AI adoption
Vendors that cannot show measurable AI-driven ROI will lose budget to those that can
Net IT budgets will grow modestly, but we expect that growth to be concentrated in applied AI and automation spending
