AI & SaaS Considerations

AI Divergence By Category and By Segment

AI adoption is reshaping SaaS category dynamics, vendors positioned as systems of action are pulling ahead in both enterprise adoption and budget allocation.

Category Divergence
Highest Upside
Systems of Action
Capture disproportionate levels of value as AI directly drives outcomes and decisions
Platforms closest to "doing the work" benefit the most from AI driven efficiency
Value creation compounds as models learn from execution data and closed loop feedback
Steady State
Systems of Record
Slower disruption, AI improves efficiency but limits incremental platform differentiation
Data integrity, compliance, and reliability requirements constrain AI driven change
AI enhances workflows rather than instituting step function product changes
⚠ High Risk
Systems of Engagement
Most crowded and dangerous place to be in 2026, AI commoditizes the surface layer quickly
Sustainable differentiation requires deep workflow integration and measurable ROI
GenAI rapidly commoditizes surface level engagement features (e.g., content, UX)
Vendors with hybrid exposure and opportunities across categories will navigate the transition most effectively

The Investor Lens

AI Diligence
Framework

Evaluating structural risk and upside in an AI driven market. Four dimensions that separate durable platforms from vulnerable ones.

Dimension & Focus Evaluation Criteria
01

Strategic Positioning

Is AI value creating or defensive for this business?
→Clear thesis for how AI drives revenue, margin, or retention
→Defined use cases tied to measurable outcomes
→Evidence of real economic impact from AI products
02

Operational Readiness

Can the company execute an AI strategy at scale?
→Clean, governed, workflow relevant data
→Evidence of efficiency gains or margin improvement
→Architecture capable of supporting agentic workflows
03

Product & Moat Durability

Does AI strengthen or weaken defensibility?
→AI embedded into core workflows, not surface level features
→Proprietary, outcome linked data assets
→Data advantage that grows with usage, not static
04

Market Exposure & Competitive Dynamics

Is the business structurally exposed or advantaged?
→Replaceable labor vs mission critical workflow positioning
→Speed of AI native entrants in the category
→Ability to consolidate vs risk of being displaced
AI diligence should assess potential for both disruption risk and clear advantage
